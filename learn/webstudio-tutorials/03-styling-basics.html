The Style Panel

When you click on any element, the Style Panel appears on the right side. This is where the magic happens!

The Style Panel lets you change everything about how your element looks - colors, sizes, spacing, and more!

Remember: Click on the element you want to change first, then use the Style Panel to make changes. Your changes will only affect the element you selected!
2

Colors - Make It Pop!

Colors make your website fun and eye-catching! You can change:

  • Text Color - The color of words
  • Background Color - The color behind things
  • Border Color - The color of outlines

How to change colors:

  1. Click on an element
  2. Find "Color" or "Background" in the Style Panel
  3. Click the color box to open the color picker
  4. Choose your favorite color!

Size - Big and Small

You can make things bigger or smaller! This is called changing the "size".

Small Text
Normal Text
Big Text
Huge!

Size options:

  • Font Size - How big the letters are
  • Width - How wide something is
  • Height - How tall something is
Tip: You can use different units for sizes! "px" means pixels (like 24px), "%" means percent of the container (like 50%), and "rem" is relative to the base font size.

Spacing - Room to Breathe

Spacing makes things look clean and organized. There are two types:

Padding - Space INSIDE an element

Lots of padding around me!

Margin - Space OUTSIDE an element

I have margin pushing things away!
Easy Way to Remember:
  • Padding = Space inside (like stuffing in a pillow)
  • Margin = Space outside (like a fence around a house)

Borders - Outline Your Elements

Borders are lines around your elements. They can make things stand out!

Border options:

  • Border Width - How thick the line is
  • Border Style - Solid, dashed, or dotted
  • Border Color - What color the line is
  • Border Radius - How round the corners are!
