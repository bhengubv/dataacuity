Headings - Big Titles

Headings are the big, bold titles on your page. They tell visitors what each section is about!

What a Heading looks like:
This is a Heading!

How to add a Heading:

  1. Find "Heading" in the left panel
  2. Drag it onto your canvas
  3. Double-click to type your own text
Pro Tip: Headings come in different sizes! H1 is the biggest (for main titles), H2 is medium, and H3 is smaller. You can change this in the style panel!
2

Text - Paragraphs and Words

Text elements are for writing paragraphs - the regular text that tells your story!

What Text looks like:
This is regular text. You can write as much as you want here! It's perfect for describing things, telling stories, or sharing information with your visitors.

How to add Text:

  1. Find "Paragraph" or "Text" in the left panel
  2. Drag it under your heading
  3. Double-click and start typing!
3

Images - Pictures and Photos

Images make your website fun and colorful! You can add photos, drawings, or any picture you like.

Images can be any picture:
Your Image Here!

How to add an Image:

  1. Find "Image" in the left panel
  2. Drag it onto your canvas
  3. Click on the image to open settings
  4. Click "Add Image" to upload your picture
Tip: You can also use images from the internet by pasting a URL! Ask a parent or teacher for help finding good images.
4

Buttons - Clickable Things

Buttons are interactive elements that visitors can click! They can go to other pages, send emails, or do other cool things.

What a Button looks like:
Click Me!

How to add a Button:

  1. Find "Button" in the left panel
  2. Drag it onto your canvas
  3. Double-click to change the text
  4. Use the settings panel to add a link
5

Boxes - Containers for Grouping

Boxes are invisible containers that help you organize things! They're like frames that hold other elements together.

A Box containing other elements:
Inside a Box!
This text is also inside the same box.
Button in box
Why use Boxes? Boxes help you:
  • Group related things together
  • Create layouts (like columns)
  • Add backgrounds and borders to sections

Challenge: Build Your About Me Page!

Challenge Time! Create a simple "About Me" page using what you learned:
  1. Add a big heading: "About Me" or your name
  2. Add an image (maybe a picture you like!)
  3. Add a paragraph about yourself
  4. Add a button that says "My Favorite Things"

Don't worry about making it look perfect - we'll learn how to style everything in the next lesson!
