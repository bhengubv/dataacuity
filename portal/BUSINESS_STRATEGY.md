## Executive Summary

DataAcuity is a **unified data intelligence platform** providing businesses with enterprise-grade tools for analytics, automation, CRM, and AI - all hosted with full data sovereignty. We eliminate the complexity and cost of managing multiple SaaS subscriptions by offering an integrated suite of open-source tools under a single platform.

**Value Proposition:** "Enterprise data intelligence at 70% less than competitors, priced in ZAR."

**FOREX Advantage:** International customers benefit from favorable exchange rates:
- R500/month ≈ $27 USD ≈ £22 GBP ≈ €25 EUR
- Comparable tools cost $100-300/user/month internationally

### What's Included in Each Tier

#### Free Tier (R0/month)
*"Experience the full power of one workflow"*

- Access to ONE complete end-to-end process of your choice:
  - **Option A:** CRM → Lead capture, pipeline, basic reporting
  - **Option B:** Analytics → Connect 1 data source, 3 dashboards
  - **Option C:** Automation → 1 workflow, 100 executions/month
  - **Option D:** AI → 50 conversations/month, 1 model
- Bio Pages (1 page)
- File Converter (20 conversions/month)
- Community support (forum, documentation)
- Branded "Powered by DataAcuity"

#### Starter (R499/month)
*"Everything you need to run your business"*

- **1 user** with full platform access
- All applications unlocked:
  - CRM (unlimited contacts, deals)
  - Analytics (unlimited dashboards)
  - Workflows (unlimited, 5,000 executions/mo)
  - AI Assistant (all models, 500 conversations/mo)
  - Data Sync (3 connections)
  - File Converter (500 conversions/mo)
  - Bio Pages (10 pages)
  - Markets Dashboard
- Email support (48hr response)
- No "Powered by" branding
- Full data export

#### Growth (R499 + R99/user, up to 10 users)
*"Scale your team without scaling your costs"*

Everything in Starter, plus:
- **Up to 10 users** (R499 base + R99 per additional user)
- Example: 5 users = R499 + (4 × R99) = **R895/month**
- Example: 10 users = R499 + (9 × R99) = **R1,390/month**
- Increased limits:
  - Workflows: 25,000 executions/mo
  - AI: 2,500 conversations/mo
  - Data Sync: 10 connections
  - File Converter: unlimited
- Team collaboration features
- Shared dashboards & workflows
- Priority email support (24hr response)
- Quarterly usage reports

#### Option A: "Sales Machine"
Complete CRM experience:
- Lead capture forms

- Deal pipeline (1 pipeline)
- Basic activity tracking
- 3 email templates
- Simple reporting

#### Option B: "Data Insights"
Complete analytics experience:
- 1 data source connection
- 3 custom dashboards
- Scheduled reports (weekly)
- Basic SQL access
- Export to PDF/Excel

#### Option C: "Automation Engine"
Complete workflow experience:
- 1 active workflow
- 20 executions/month
- Access to all 400+ integrations
- Webhook triggers
- Email notifications

#### Option D: "AI Analyst"

- 50 conversations/month

- Basic document analysis
- Export conversations
